# Sales Manager Dashboard Specification – HubSpot

## Principle
Outcome and governance metrics take precedence over activity metrics.

## Dashboards
1. Sales Manager – Pipeline & Governance
2. SDR Activity & Call Quality – Coaching

## Pipeline Health
- Deals by Stage
- Conversion Rates
- Time in Stage

## Governance
- Deals without authority
- Pricing without demo
- At risk or stalled

## Forecast
- Decision Pending
- Timeline breakdown
- Won vs Lost

## Discounts & Handover
- Deals with discounts
- Without approval
- Won without handover

## Activity Dashboard (Coaching Only)
- Calls
- Emails
- Meetings
- Minutes

Use trends only. Not for ranking.

## Review
Weekly governance + coaching reviews.
