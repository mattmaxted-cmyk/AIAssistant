# Nomos Group Marketing Plan 2026

## Overview

This marketing plan covers all digital marketing, lead generation, and brand awareness initiatives for Nomos Group. The strategy targets two distinct ICPs: B2C (high-net-worth homeowners commissioning luxury builds) and B2B (professionals who refer, specify, or commission luxury construction work).

---

## ICP Definitions

### B2C ICP ? "The Homeowner"
High-net-worth women aged 30+ who are decision-makers or key influencers in luxury residential build projects. Reached via Meta (Instagram/Facebook) and the Nomos website.

### B2B ICP ? "The Professional Referrer" (LinkedIn)
Smaller, luxury-focused professional firms. These are people who either commission work directly, refer clients, or influence build decisions professionally. Target on LinkedIn via Neo's organic content and Dripify outreach.

| Persona | Why They Matter |
|---|---|
| **Luxury architects** (smaller practices) | Specify contractors for high-end residential projects ? a relationship with the right architect = repeat pipeline |
| **Quantity surveyors** (luxury/residential focus) | Involved in procurement and contractor selection on high-value projects |
| **Property managers** (premium/luxury portfolio) | Manage ongoing maintenance and refurbishment projects for HNW clients |
| **High-end estate agents** | Work closely with HNW buyers and vendors who often commission build or renovation work |

**Key targeting criteria for Sales Navigator:**
- Smaller firms (not large corporates ? we want boutique, premium-focused practices)
- UK-based
- Residential / luxury / high-end focus where identifiable
- Job titles: Architect, Director, Associate, QS, Property Manager, Estate Agent (senior roles)

## 4. Bespoke Offering Terminology

### ? Confirmed ? 18 March 2026

**Definition:** Any project or engagement that requires design work is classified as **Bespoke**.

**Terminology rule:** Always refer to this offering as **"Bespoke"** ? no alternative names. This applies across all marketing materials, the website, HubSpot pipeline naming, and sales conversations.

**Differentiation:** If a lead requires design work ? Bespoke pipeline. If a lead is coming to Nomos with design already complete and only needs project delivery ? Project Only pipeline.

## 8. HubSpot CRM Setup

### Account
HubSpot account already created (Stelios's account). Matt to configure.

### Pipeline Structure

**Pipeline 1: Design Service** (lead has no architect ť Nomos provides design + build)

| Stage | Notes |
|---|---|
| Qualified | Passed qualification call |
| Site Visit Completed | Assessed the project on site |
| Design Proposal Sent | Design fee/concept proposal delivered |
| Design Proposal Approval | Client signs off on design |
| Build Proposal Sent | Construction quote delivered |
| Closed Won | Contract signed, project starts |
| Closed Lost | Didn't proceed |

**Pipeline 2: Project Only** (lead has architect ť build only)

| Stage | Notes |
|---|---|
| Qualified | Passed qualification call |
| Site Visit Completed | Assessed the project on site |
| Proposal Sent | Build quote delivered |
| Closed Won | Contract signed, project starts |
| Closed Lost | Didn't proceed |

> Note: "Bespoke" is the terminology used in all marketing materials and client-facing communications for the Design Service pipeline. The HubSpot pipeline is named "Design Service" for internal clarity.

### Lead Flow
1. Contact enters HubSpot (from website form, ads, or manual entry)
2. Qualifying call conducted
3. Based on call outcome:
   - Route to Design Service pipeline
   - Route to Project Only pipeline
   - Disqualify
