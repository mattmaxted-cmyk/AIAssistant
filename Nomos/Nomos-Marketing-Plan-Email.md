### What we're doing
Running a Meta ad campaign targeting our B2C ICP: affluent women aged 30+ who influence or make decisions on luxury home builds. The campaign uses a top-of-funnel video ad to drive traffic to a dedicated landing page, where a second video converts them.

### Why
This audience researches and often initiates the conversation about building a luxury home. We need to reach them where they spend time (Instagram/Facebook) with aspirational content that positions Nomos as the premium choice.

### Target ICP
**"Housewife 30+"** ? High-net-worth women aged 30+ who are decision-makers or key influencers in luxury residential build projects.

## 4. Bespoke Offering Terminology

### Confirmed ? 18 March 2026

**Definition:** Any project or engagement that requires design work is classified as **Bespoke**.

**Terminology rule:** Always refer to this as **"Bespoke"** ? no alternative names. This applies across all marketing materials, the website, HubSpot pipeline naming, and all sales conversations.

**How to route leads:**
- Requires design work ? **Bespoke pipeline**
- Design already complete, project delivery only ? **Project Only pipeline**

## 8. HubSpot CRM Setup

### What we're doing
Setting up a free HubSpot CRM instance for Nomos using Stelios's account. Two deal pipelines: one for design/bespoke services and one for project-only work. Leads from the website and ads enter HubSpot, get a qualifying call, then are routed to the appropriate pipeline or disqualified.

### Why
We need a proper CRM to track leads and deals. The two-pipeline structure reflects the two different service paths Nomos offers.

### Lead Flow
1. Contact enters HubSpot (website form, Meta ads, manual entry)
2. Qualifying call conducted
3. Based on outcome:
   - Route to **Bespoke pipeline**
   - Route to **Project Only pipeline**
   - Or **disqualify** if not a fit

| HubSpot configuration | Matt | HubSpot account already created. Matt to set up contact views, company views, and deal pipelines |

Neo to define remaining stages from enquiry to signed contract. For **Project Only pipeline**: same question. Matt needs this before he can build the pipelines in HubSpot |

## 9. Planning Application Outreach (Cold Email)

### What we're doing
Automated weekly outreach to planning application leads. We receive a CSV of planning applications, filter by budget (Ż200k+), then AI extracts and formats the personalised information from the CSV (project details, location, etc.) into columns that Instantly uses as merge fields. Leads are pushed into two Instantly email campaigns ? one for clients/applicants and one for architects/agents.

### Why
Planning applications are a goldmine of qualified leads. People who've just submitted planning for a Ż200k+ project will need a contractor. We reach them (and their architect) with personalised, relevant outreach.

### Prerequisites
We need multiple Nomos domains set up for cold outbound to protect the primary domain reputation and enable sending at scale.
