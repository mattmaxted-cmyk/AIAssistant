### `/lp/certify-pay-less-notice`

**Status:** Active  
**Platform:** Google Search  
**Ad Group:** Pay Less Notice  
**Starting CPC:** £5.00  
**Monthly search volume:** ~270 searches/month (after removing generic "pay less notice" term)  

**What this page is:** Dedicated landing page for pay less notice search terms. Audience is commercial teams searching for how to manage pay less notice deadlines — very high intent, but some informational query risk.

**Page requirements (Quality Score alignment):**
- Headline: "Never Miss a Pay Less Notice Deadline Again"
- Construction Act legislation referenced explicitly (HGCRA, Section 111, prescribed period)
- Pain block: consequences of missing a deadline (full gross sum due, no comeback)
- Feature-to-benefit table: notice tracking, auto-calculated windows, deadline alerts, audit trail
- Single CTA: "Book a Demo"
- Key terms on page: "pay less notice", "Construction Act", "HGCRA", "Section 111", "payment notice"

**Key keywords driving traffic:**
- "pay less notice template" (70/mo — high intent)
- "JCT pay less notice" (70/mo — borderline, monitor)
- "pay less notice construction act" (70/mo — borderline, monitor)
- "pay less notice construction" (30/mo — borderline, monitor)
- "JCT pay less notice template" (20/mo — high intent)
- "pay less notice software" (no data — included for intent coverage)

**What is being tested:** Borderline keywords (JCT terms, Construction Act terms) are being monitored for irrelevant query patterns. If bounce rate on JCT terms is materially higher than the ad group average, those keywords will be paused. Review at first monthly search terms report.

## Notes & Conventions

- All `/lp/` pages are **no-indexed** — they do not appear in organic search
- All pages use HubSpot forms — leads route to the relevant pipeline
- UTM parameters required on all destination URLs — see UTM parameter rules
- LinkedIn Insight Tag must be firing on the demo form thank-you page for conversion tracking
- Google Ads linked to GA4 — keyword-level bounce rate visible via GA4 Explorations (dimension: Session Google Ads keyword)
- Do not use Google PMax for Certify campaigns — manual CPC only during early data-gathering phase
- Certify Google pages: one dedicated page per ad group — do not send both ad groups to the same page
