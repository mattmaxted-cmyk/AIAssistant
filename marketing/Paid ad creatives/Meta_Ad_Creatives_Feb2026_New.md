# StoneRise Meta Ad Creatives - February 2026

**Client:** StoneRise  
**Campaign Type:** Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)  
**Dimensions:** 1000 x 1000 pixels (single image)  
**Target Audience:** Commercial & Financial Teams (Director level) at UK Construction sub-contractors and main contractors

---

## Design Guidelines (from Brief)

- **Priority:** Strong copy that is easily readable with high contrast to background
- **Branding:** Use StoneRise website branding
- **Style:** Clean, modern, construction-focused

## Notes for Design Team

1. **Typography:** Use bold, sans-serif fonts for headlines. Ensure minimum 40pt font size for primary copy to be readable on mobile.

2. **Colour palette:** Follow StoneRise brand guidelines. Use high contrast between text and background (dark text on light, or white text on dark branded backgrounds).

3. **Format:** All ads 1000 x 1000px for Meta feed placements. Consider creating 1080 x 1920px variants for Stories/Reels.

4. **Branding:** Include StoneRise logo in corner of every ad (bottom right preferred).

5. **Mobile-first:** Design for mobile viewing — test legibility at small sizes.
