## Winning Creative Analysis

Based on campaign performance, these 3 creatives are winning (Meta pushing them):

### Winner 1: Notes App Checklist
- **Format:** iPhone Notes app UI
- **Hook:** "How to manage construction procurement"
- **Structure:** Pain points crossed out (❌) + solution with green tick (✓)
- **Why it works:** Native format, problem→solution, scannable list

### Winner 2: Myth-Busting Quote
- **Format:** Clean design with bold quote
- **Hook:** "Construction procurement can't be automated"
- **Structure:** Quote the objection → disprove with stat
- **Why it works:** Pattern interrupt, contrarian, challenges assumptions

### Winner 3: WhatsApp Conversation
- **Format:** WhatsApp chat UI
- **Hook:** Peer asking "How do you manage procurement without spreadsheets?"
- **Structure:** Question → Simple answer → "Signing up now!"
- **Why it works:** Native format, social proof, conversational, FOMO

### Winning Patterns to Replicate
1. **Native UI formats** (Notes, WhatsApp, iMessage, Email, Slack)
2. **Problem → Solution** structure
3. **Single clear stat or proof point**
4. **Conversational/peer-to-peer tone**
5. **Minimal, clean design**
6. **Pattern interrupts** (myths, questions, quotes)

## Design Guidelines

### Format Templates (in order of priority)

1. **Notes App** — iPhone Notes UI, yellow header, checklist with ❌ and ✓
2. **WhatsApp** — Green header, chat bubbles, timestamps, "online" status
3. **iMessage** — Blue/grey bubbles, contact name at top, timestamps
4. **Slack** — Purple sidebar implied, channel name, usernames with timestamps
5. **Myth-Bust** — Clean white/light grey background, StoneRise logo top centre, bold quote in quotation marks, counter-stat below

### Typography
- Notes/Chat formats: Use system fonts (SF Pro, Segoe UI) to look authentic
- Myth-bust format: Bold sans-serif for quote, regular weight for proof point
- Minimum 36pt for primary text on mobile

### Colour Palette
- Follow StoneRise brand (green accents)
- Chat formats: Match native app colours exactly
- Myth-bust: White background, dark text, green for StoneRise branding

### Critical Rules
1. **Native formats must look authentic** — match the real app UI exactly
2. **One stat per ad** — don't overload with multiple proof points
3. **Mobile-first** — all text must be readable at small sizes
4. **Logo placement:** Top centre for myth-bust format; not needed for chat formats (StoneRise mentioned in conversation)

---

## Caption Templates

All captions should follow this structure:
1. **Hook question or pain point** (first line, most important)
2. **Solution + benefit**
3. **Implied CTA**

Keep under 125 characters for the primary text that shows above "...more"
