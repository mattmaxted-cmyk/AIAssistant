### Typography

| Usage | Font (Print) | Font (Online) |
|-------|--------------|---------------|
| Body copy | Avenir Roman | Inter Regular |
| Headings | Avenir Roman or Heavy | Inter Regular or Bold |
| Highlights | Avenir Bold | Inter Bold |

- Minimum body copy: 9pt
- Bullet points: circular, matching font size, in a StoneRise brand colour

## Quick Reference: PowerPoint Setup

1. **Slide size:** Design → Slide Size → Widescreen (16:9)
2. **Theme colours:** Add the 4 StoneRise colours to your palette
3. **Fonts:** Set Avenir (or Inter) as theme fonts
4. **Master slides:** Create these masters:
   - White (white background, dark text)
   - Dark Blue (blue background, white text)
   - Dark Green (green background, white text)
   - Light Blue (light blue background, white text)
   - Split (50/50 left colour, right white)
5. **Logo:** Use Slide Master for consistent placement
