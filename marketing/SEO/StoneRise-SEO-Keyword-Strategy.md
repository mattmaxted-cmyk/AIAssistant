## Content Quality Guidelines

For every blog post:

1. **Minimum 1,500 words** for pillar content, 800-1,200 for supporting posts
2. **Include construction-specific examples** — reference real scenarios contractors face
3. **Use approved proof points** — 3 minutes invoice processing, 20% time saved, etc.
4. **Internal linking** — link to related blog posts and pillar pages
5. **Call to action** — every post should end with a relevant CTA (demo, guide download, etc.)
6. **Schema markup** — FAQ schema for question-based posts, HowTo schema for guides
