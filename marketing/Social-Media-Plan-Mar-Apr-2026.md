## Executive Summary

### What This Plan Covers

A 9-week social media strategy for March–April 2026 focused on:

1. **LinkedIn Network Building** — Michael and Stelios proactively connecting with their target personas via Sales Navigator
2. **LinkedIn Content** — Targeted posts from Michael, Stelios, and Company page to engage the network
3. **LinkedIn Lead Generation** — Scraping post engagers and running message sequences to book calls
4. **Video Content** — 30-second clips from the founders Q&A video, one per week on personal profiles (alternating) + one per week on company page
5. **Blog Content (SEO)** — High-level blogs targeting ICP pain points, posted on the Company page and reposted by Michael or Stelios based on persona overlap
6. **Brand Visibility** — Facebook and Instagram presence with repurposed content
7. **Testing** — Image carousel format test on LinkedIn

**1. Network Building (Connections)**

Michael and Stelios each run automated connection requests via PhantomBuster, targeting their specific personas from Sales Navigator searches.

```
Sales Navigator Search → PhantomBuster Auto-Connect → Accept → Network grows with ICP contacts
```

**2. Lead Generation (Engagement Scraping)**

Separate flow that scrapes people who engage with LinkedIn posts (likes, comments) and runs a message sequence.

```
LinkedIn Posts → Engagement → PhantomBuster Scrape → Filter → Approve → Message Sequence → Intro Call
```

**3. Post Boosting (Ad-Hoc)**

Some LinkedIn posts are boosted (paid promotion) for extra visibility. This is done ad-hoc based on post performance or strategic importance — not scheduled in advance.

### Blog Content — SEO & LinkedIn Distribution

Blogs written for SEO will be shared on LinkedIn to extend reach.

**Important:** Blog shares are **ad-hoc and additional** to the regular posting schedule. They do not count towards the 2 posts/week for Michael and Stelios — they are posted on top of the planned content when blogs go live.

**Distribution Flow:**
1. Blog published on stonerise.tech
2. Company page shares with summary + link
3. Michael OR Stelios reposts (based on topic alignment)

**Repost Assignment:**

| Blog Topic | Reposted By | Reason |
|------------|-------------|--------|
| Commercial workflows, variations, QS processes | Michael | Commercial/Procurement persona overlap |
| ROI, business case, cost savings, scaling | Stelios | Finance/Operations persona overlap |
| General construction tech, industry trends | Either | Whoever has capacity that week |

**Guidelines:**
- Blog shares are **in addition to** the 2 posts/week — not a replacement
- Reposts should include a personal take (1-2 sentences) rather than just sharing silently
- Space blog shares at least 2 days from regular posts to avoid saturation
- Track clicks via UTM parameters on blog links
