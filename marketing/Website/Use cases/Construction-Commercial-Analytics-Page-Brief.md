### 2. Problem Block — 3 Pain Points
Present as short, punchy statements. Each should feel like something the reader recognises from their own day:

- Project financial reports are compiled manually from multiple spreadsheets at month end. The data is always a few weeks old and decisions are made on figures that no longer reflect reality
- Without a live view of costs committed versus costs remaining, commercial teams cannot identify margin erosion until it has already happened
- Cash flow forecasting is done manually in Excel at board level. Nobody has confidence in the numbers and the board is always working from the last valuation rather than the current position

### 3. Solution Block — How Certify Works
Four features, each as a heading plus one sentence of benefit:

- Live margin dashboards — real-time project financials showing revenue, costs committed, costs remaining and current margin at a glance, without waiting for month end
- Cash flow forecasting — Certify projects income and expenditure forward based on live contract data, giving finance teams a view of the business position that is always current
- Cost to complete analysis — each project shows the gap between work done and work remaining, including uncommitted costs, so commercial exposure is always visible
- Performance reporting — summary views across all live projects allow finance and commercial directors to identify which contracts are performing and which need intervention
