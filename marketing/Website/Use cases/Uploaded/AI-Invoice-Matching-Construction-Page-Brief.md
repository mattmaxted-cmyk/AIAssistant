### 2. Problem Block — 3 Pain Points
Present as short, punchy statements. Each should feel like something the reader recognises from their own day:

- Manually matching invoices to purchase orders and delivery records across hundreds of orders a month is a full-time job. When the volume is high, the checks get faster and errors get through
- Invoices that do not match are queried by email, which means the resolution thread is disconnected from the original invoice. Disputes take weeks to resolve and the same arguments come up again next month
- When invoices are approved without being matched to a confirmed delivery, money goes out for materials that were never received or were delivered short. By the time someone notices, recovering it from the supplier is a battle

### 3. Solution Block — How StoneRise Procurement Works
Four features, each as a heading plus one sentence of benefit:

- AI invoice processing — StoneRise reads supplier invoices using OCR, extracts line items automatically and matches them against the corresponding purchase order and delivery record without manual data entry
- Three-way matching — every invoice is checked against the original PO and the confirmed delivery note, so discrepancies in quantity, price or specification are flagged before any approval is given
- Invoice query management — when a discrepancy is found, a structured query is raised against the invoice and tracked through to resolution, keeping everything in one place rather than lost in an email thread
- Batch processing — invoices can be uploaded in bulk, so finance teams can process a month's worth of supplier invoices in a fraction of the time it would take manually
