### 2. Problem Block — 3 Pain Points
Present as short, punchy statements. Each should feel like something the reader recognises from their own day:

- Purchase orders raised manually by email have no standard format, no spec attached and no way to know if the supplier has actually read them. Disputes start before the materials have even arrived on site
- Without a central record of all open orders, finance teams spend hours at month end trying to reconcile what has been committed against what has been invoiced
- When there is no audit trail connecting an order to an approval, spend goes unchallenged. Orders are raised without the right authority and nobody finds out until the invoice arrives

### 3. Solution Block — How StoneRise Procurement Works
Four features, each as a heading plus one sentence of benefit:

- Automated PO creation — purchase orders are generated automatically from approved requests, with full line item detail, project codes and specs attached, removing the need for manual document creation
- PO tracking — every open order shows current status from raised to delivered, so procurement and finance teams always know where a commitment stands
- Project cost allocation — each PO is coded to a project and sub-code at line item level, so committed costs are allocated correctly from the moment the order is placed
- Complete audit trail — every PO is linked to the original request and the approval that authorised it, creating a full chain of evidence that survives any dispute
