## Purpose

This page covers Certify's complete variation management capability for main contractors: downstream (subcontractor variations coming in) and upstream (client variations going out). The two workflows are linked — approved subcontractor variations automatically generate the corresponding client variation with markup applied. Keep scope tight to variation management only. Do not reference payment applications, procurement, HR or any other StoneRise module.

### 2. Problem Block — 3 Pain Points
Present as short, punchy statements. Each should feel like something the reader recognises from their own day:

- Subcontractor variation submissions arrive with no consistent format, no supporting evidence and figures that bear no relation to the subcontract scope. Without a structured process, every claim becomes a negotiation that takes hours to resolve
- Client variations are tracked across 20 or 30 live packages in spreadsheets. Approved variations miss the next valuation and the revenue is not recovered until the final account. By then it is a dispute rather than an admin fix
- The link between subcontractor cost and client recovery is managed manually, if at all. Markup calculations are done in spreadsheets. When the connection breaks, margin leaks out and nobody notices until the final account

### 3. Solution Block — How Certify Works
Four features, each as a heading plus one sentence of benefit:

- Structured subcontractor submission portal — subcontractors submit variations through a guided form that requires supporting evidence, subcontract reference and pricing breakdown, so invalid claims are filtered out before a QS reviews them
- AI variation assessment — Certify reviews each subcontractor submission against the subcontract documents and spec, identifies claims outside scope and flags overpriced items before commercial time is spent on them
- Upstream variation link — when a subcontractor variation is approved, Certify automatically generates the corresponding client variation with the correct markup applied, so every downstream cost has an upstream recovery
- Complete variation register — every variation on both sides of the contract is logged, tracked and auditable from instruction to final decision, giving commercial teams a clear position at any point in the project
