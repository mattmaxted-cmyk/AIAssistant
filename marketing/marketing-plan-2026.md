# StoneRise Technology — Marketing Plan 2026

## Executive Summary

This plan covers multi-channel marketing for StoneRise Technology, targeting construction contractors across the UK. The strategy leverages founder credibility, industry pain points, and product-led content across LinkedIn, Meta, Google, email, and supporting channels.

**Primary Target:** Main contractors (commercial decision flows downstream, forcing subcontractor adoption)
**Secondary Target:** Subcontractors and SME contractors seeking point solutions
**Live Products:** Procurement App, HR App
**Coming Soon:** Commercial/QS App (major differentiator)

**Post 9: Technical Architecture**

> Something a bit nerdy: our approach to multi-tenancy.
>
> Every StoneRise customer's data is completely isolated. Same infrastructure, but strict separation at the database level. Your data never touches another company's data.
>
> Why does this matter?
>
> For one, security. But also performance and reliability. One customer doing a massive export doesn't slow down everyone else.
>
> We also built it so your data is portable. If you ever need to leave (hopefully you won't!), you can export everything. No lock-in.
>
> Not the sexiest feature to talk about. But it's the kind of foundational decision that matters when you're trusting a system with your business operations.
>
> Building properly from day one beats patching things later.

**Post 3: Cost of Manual Processes**

> Quick exercise: work out what your procurement admin actually costs.
>
> Take your average purchase order volume. Multiply by time spent per PO — request, approval, chasing, receipting, invoice matching. Add the hourly cost of the people doing it.

**Core Characteristics:**
- Commercially and financially focused
- ROI-driven, business case oriented
- Numbers-led — quantifies costs, savings, returns
- Speaking to MDs, FDs, and financial decision-makers
- Confident authority on business efficiency

**Tone:**
- Professional and measured
- Less technical than Michael, more strategic
- Frames everything through financial impact
- Direct about costs and returns
- Credible — speaks from experience running commercial operations

**Common Phrases:**
- "The numbers speak for themselves..."
- "Work out what this actually costs..."
- "The ROI on this is..."
- "If [problem] is costing you money, let's talk..."
- "From my time as Finance Director..."

**Topics:**
- Hidden costs in construction operations
- Admin overhead and wasted time
- Payment errors and margin leakage
- Cash flow visibility
- Scaling without adding headcount
- Cost of fragmented systems
- ROI calculations and payback periods

**Differentiator from Michael:**
- Michael: Creative, product-led, excited about features and tech, softer tone, user experience focus
- Stelios: Commercial, ROI-focused, cost savings, business benefits, financial justification
