## Elevator Pitch

StoneRise is an automated procurement tool that helps commercial and financial teams in construction compare quotes for materials, manage external suppliers and consolidate spending in one digital workflow.

Construction firms currently use a fragmented, manual process for procurement that results in overspend, poor supplier visibility, compliance gaps and costly delays.

StoneRise brings the whole procurement process into one easy workflow. Instead of chasing suppliers, digging through emails and dealing with paperwork, you get better prices, faster approvals and fewer problems on site.

## Problem Summary

Construction firms currently use a fragmented, manual process for procurement that results in overspend, poor supplier visibility, compliance gaps and costly delays.

### Sub-Problems

| # | Sub-Problem |
|---|------------|
| 1 | **Overspend & pricing inconsistencies** — Using manual, disparate systems for procurement leads to overspend and pricing inconsistencies |
| 2 | **Admin overload & errors** — Paperwork and manual POs create admin overload, error and invoice disputes |
| 3 | **Slow, inconsistent approvals** — Approvals are slow, inconsistent and vulnerable to bottlenecks or mistakes |
| 4 | **Zero visibility** — Zero visibility on the status of requests and deliveries causes delays, delivery disputes and compliance failures |

## Differentiation Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|------------|
| **Centralised Multi-Supplier Management** | Multi-supplier RFQ engine that groups items and sends them to several suppliers instantly |
| **Automated PO Creation** | Auto-build POs from approved requests; attach drawings & specs, and consolidate orders |
| **Rule-Based Approvals** | Custom approval chains, routing rules, role-based permission and escalation logic |
| **Real-Time Order Tracking** | Status updates, mandatory delivery photos, plant/waste automation and audit logs |

## Messaging Hierarchy

Use this hierarchy to structure any piece of marketing content:

1. **Lead with the problem** — fragmented, manual procurement causing overspend, delays, errors
2. **Introduce the category** — construction procurement software
3. **State the solution** — one digital workflow for the entire procurement process
4. **Reinforce with pillars** — multi-supplier management, automated POs, rule-based approvals, real-time tracking
5. **Prove with benefits/stats** — the quantified outcomes listed above
6. **Close with differentiation summary** — better prices, faster approvals, fewer problems on site
